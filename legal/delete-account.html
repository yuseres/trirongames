Delete Account

Developer: Claudiu Tanasa  ·  Last updated: July 1, 2026

This page covers account deletion for any app published by TrironGames that lets you sign in (for example, Color Boom, which uses Google Sign-In to save your progress across devices). If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so either directly in the app or by email — see the two options below.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app

Recommended where available Fastest

Most TrironGames apps that support accounts follow the same pattern — in Color Boom, for example:

  1. Open the app and tap your profile / avatar icon on the main menu.
  2. Tap Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

The exact menu wording can vary slightly between apps, but look for an Account or Profile section in Settings. Your account is immediately scheduled for deletion and is permanently removed after a 7‑day grace period. You can cancel at any time during those 7 days by returning to the same screen and tapping Cancel Deletion — your account and progress will be kept exactly as they were.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Use this option if you no longer have the app installed, if it doesn't have an in-app deletion option, or if you'd rather have us process the request for you.

Send an email to trirongames@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request – [app name] (replace [app name] with the app you're using, e.g. Color Boom), and include:

  • The name of the app your account is with.
  • The Google account email address you used to sign in.
  • Your in-app Account ID (UID), if you still have the app installed. You can usually find it by tapping your profile icon → Account — it's the short code shown just below your sign-in method. Accounts are stored and looked up by this ID rather than by email, so including it lets us locate and delete your data much faster.
  • A brief confirmation that you want your account and data deleted.
Email requests are processed within 7 business days, and we'll send a confirmation to your email address once deletion is complete. In-app requests (Option 1) are processed automatically once the 7‑day grace period ends.

What data is deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed:

  • Your game progress and level data
  • Your display name and score on the leaderboard, for apps that have one
  • Your Google account identifier stored in our systems
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

Data that may be retained

Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (with no personal identifiers) may be retained for up to 24 months as described in our Privacy Policy. No data that can identify you personally is kept after deletion.

Revoking Google Sign-In access

You can also independently revoke an app's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions and removing it from the list of connected apps. Note that this only revokes access — it does not delete your saved data, so please also use Option 1 or 2 above if you want your data removed.

Questions or complaints

If you have any questions about account deletion, believe your data wasn't fully removed, or have any other complaint about how your request was handled, contact us first at trirongames@gmail.com and we'll do our best to resolve it directly. If you're not satisfied with our response, you may also refer to the complaint options listed in our Privacy Policy.
