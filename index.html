Who we are

TrironGames started as a joke — a not-so-serious “what if we just made a game?” between friends that refused to go away. We kept poking at it, one scrappy prototype at a time, until it stuck. Now the three of us make games because it's simply how we like to spend our time, and Color Boom is the first thing we're proud enough to share. This is only the beginning.
