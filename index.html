Who we are

TrironGames started as a joke — a not-so-serious “what if we just made a game?” that refused to go away. We kept poking at it, one scrappy prototype at a time, until it stuck. Now making games is simply part of how we spend our time, and Color Boom is the first thing we're proud enough to share. This is only the beginning.

Claudiu Tănasă Dev / Creator
