Delete Account – Color Boom

Developer: Claudiu Tanasa  ·  Last updated: June 8, 2026

Color Boom lets you sign in with your Google account to save your game progress across devices. If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to tanasaclaudiu114@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request – Color Boom.

Please include the following information in your email:

  • The Google account email address you used to sign in to Color Boom
  • A brief confirmation that you want your account and data deleted
We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation to your email address once deletion is complete.

What data is deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed:

  • Your game progress and level data
  • Your Google account identifier stored in our systems
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

Data that may be retained

Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (with no personal identifiers) may be retained for up to 24 months as described in our Privacy Policy. No data that can identify you personally is kept after deletion.

Revoking Google Sign-In access

You can also independently revoke Color Boom's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions and removing Color Boom from the list of connected apps.

Contact

For any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at tanasaclaudiu114@gmail.com.
